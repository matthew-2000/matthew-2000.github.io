About

PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Salerno (from 2025). MSc in Computer Science (110/110 cum laude, 2025) and BSc in Computer Science (95/110, 2022), both from the University of Salerno. Ongoing research collaboration with HCI-USE Lab on extended reality and conversational agents. Current web development activity in the PRIN 2022 MediArSI project.

Research

AI-Powered Digital Twins

I investigate digital twin architectures for sustainable urban transformation scenarios. The goal is to produce reliable computational support for analysis and decision workflows.

Extended Reality and Adaptive Agents

I study conversational and adaptive virtual agents in immersive environments. This work addresses interaction design and evaluation for robust XR systems.

Digital Cultural Heritage Interfaces

I contribute to systems for geolocating and exploring historical art commissions in Southern Italy. The technical focus is responsive data access for research and public-facing usage.
